What is Mad Libs?

Mad Libs is a humorous word game where a story or phrase is created with blank spaces for certain elements, such as nouns, adjectives, verbs, and more. Players fill in these blanks with words of their choice, without knowing the context of the story. The resulting filled-in story is often absurd, silly, and entertaining, as the word choices create unexpected and humorous combinations.
